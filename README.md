## Overview

This repository maps the convergence of private technology infrastructure, foreign sovereign capital, and the restructuring of U.S. federal governance between 2016 and 2025. The research identifies documented temporal correlations and structural connections while maintaining strict evidentiary standards.

**Core thesis:** A cohesive network of private technology actors has integrated deeply into the federal government, while receiving systematically less congressional scrutiny than consumer-facing tech companies — despite receiving billions in government contracts.

> **Important:** This research documents correlations warranting oversight investigation. It does not accuse any individual or organization of wrongdoing. Correlations are not causation. Formal investigation with subpoena power would be required to determine intent or establish legal violations.

## The 9-Layer Asymmetric Nexus Model

This repository frames U.S. tech consolidation through nine distinct layers where public agency has been displaced by private vendor networks.

### 1. Bureaucratic Layer — Privatization of the Administrative State
- **Key entity:** Department of Government Efficiency (DOGE)
- **Mechanisms:** SGE loophole appointments, Schedule F reclassifications, vendor-aligned "ghost staff" embedded in agencies (HHS, USAID)

### 2. Judicial & Constitutional Layer
- **Key events:** *Loper Bright v. Raimondo* (overturning *Chevron* deference), Major Questions Doctrine
- **Effect:** Regulatory paralysis creating a "clear zone" for state-tech companies

### 3. AI & Infrastructure Layer — Sovereign Cloud
- **Key initiative:** [Stargate Project](https://openai.com/index/stargate-advances-with-partnership-with-oracle/) ($500B AI infrastructure)
- **The duopoly:** Defense computing consolidation into Oracle and Palantir
- **Energy nexus:** NEPA waivers for nuclear power benefiting Oklo and TerraPower investors

### 4. Financial-Monetary Layer
- **Key initiatives:** Strategic Bitcoin Reserve, Stablecoin-Treasury integration (Tether/Cantor Fitzgerald), Texas Stock Exchange (TXSE)
- **Pattern:** Crypto policy shaped by officials with portfolio exposure

### 5. Foreign & Geopolitical Layer
- **Key conflict:** [Stargate](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/01/21/trump-ai-openai-oracle-softbank.html) (UAE/Microsoft) vs. [HUMAIN](https://english.alarabiya.net/News/saudi-arabia/2025/05/12/saudi-crown-prince-launches-humain-to-position-saudi-arabia-as-a-global-ai-leader) (Saudi/xAI) AI infrastructure competition
- **Risk:** Transfer of U.S. defense-grade compute (NVIDIA H100s) to foreign sovereigns

### 6. Bio-Political Layer
- **Movement:** "Make America Healthy Again" (MAHA)
- **Mechanisms:** HSA expansion into unregulated supplements (TrueMed), genomic data privatization (23andMe)

### 7. Kinetic & Extra-Territorial Layer
- **Border:** Anduril's autonomous surveillance infrastructure ("Smart Wall")
- **Space:** SpaceX's "Free Planet" legal doctrine for Mars operations

### 8. Epistemic & Educational Layer
- **Institutions:** University of Austin (UATX), 1776 Commission
- **Media:** Ellison–Musk axis (X, Skydance/Paramount)

### 9. Theological Layer
- **Philosophy:** Techno-Integralism, Girardian mimetic theory applied to governance
- **Concept:** "Efficiency" as organizing ideology; scapegoating the "Deep State" to consolidate support

## Methodology

All claims are categorized by evidence level:

| Level | Definition | Examples |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| **DOCUMENTED** | Primary sources | FEC filings, SEC documents, court records, official announcements, USASpending.gov |
| **REPORTED** | Quality journalism with named sources | Bloomberg, WIRED, Reuters, Breaking Defense |
| **PATTERN** | Temporal or structural correlation | Timing clusters, network mapping, oversight comparisons |
| **SPECULATIVE** | Hypothesis requiring investigation | Pre-coordination theories, intent-based claims |

### Alternative Explanations Considered

- **Contract acceleration:** Palantir's legitimate technology superiority; normal military modernization timelines; defense budget increases
- **Oversight asymmetry:** Consumer tech affects more voters directly; foreign-owned TikTok raises unique concerns; contractor oversight may occur in classified settings
- **Donation–contract correlations:** Companies donate to likely winners regardless of policy expectations; standard procurement timelines; personnel with relevant expertise naturally cluster
